ABOUT US



MonMatcha café is a business focused on matcha lovers, teenagers, cafe hunters, and social media users.In our cafe, matcha is not only served in the form of warm tea brew but also processed into a variety of food and beverage dishes. Variety for our cafe, we will maintain the unique taste and fresh taste of the matcha special. Our cafe is located at No.55, Jalan SS15/8a, SS 15 Subang Jaya. Our cafe open from Monday to Saturday from 8.00 AM to 10.00 PM.
