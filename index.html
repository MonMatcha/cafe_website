Delicious Matcha Lattes require Quality Matcha

What makes our matcha special?

Our matcha is carefully grown in the garden and only the finest leaves are picked and the finest materials are used. The process involves removing the stalks and veins from the leaves and crushing them into a fine powder to make matcha.

In our cafe, matcha is not only served in the form of warm tea brew but also processed into a variety of food and beverage dishes. Variety for our cafe, we will maintain the unique taste and fresh taste of the matcha special.
